# Maintenance Schedules

## Monthly
- [ ] Check/replace HVAC filters (monthly with pets, every 2-3 months without)
- [ ] Test smoke detectors and CO detectors
- [ ] Clean kitchen exhaust fan/filter
- [ ] Check under sinks for leaks
- [ ] Run water in unused fixtures (prevents P-trap dry-out)
- [ ] Clean garbage disposal (ice + salt or vinegar + baking soda)

## Quarterly (Every 3 Months)
- [ ] Test GFCI outlets (press test/reset buttons)
- [ ] Check water softener salt level (if applicable)
- [ ] Inspect fire extinguishers
- [ ] Clean dryer vent and lint trap housing
- [ ] Check caulking around tubs, showers, and sinks
- [ ] Flush tankless water heater (if applicable)

## Spring Checklist
- [ ] Inspect roof for winter damage (from ground with binoculars, or hire inspector)
- [ ] Clean gutters and downspouts
- [ ] Check exterior drainage — water should flow away from foundation
- [ ] Service AC unit (clean coils, check refrigerant — hire HVAC tech)
- [ ] Inspect windows and doors — reseal or recaulk as needed
- [ ] Check deck/patio for winter damage, power wash
- [ ] Test outdoor faucets and irrigation system
- [ ] Inspect foundation for cracks
- [ ] Aerate and fertilize lawn (if applicable)
- [ ] Check attic for signs of leaks or pests
- [ ] Clean window screens

## Summer Checklist
- [ ] Inspect and clean outdoor grill
- [ ] Check and maintain swimming pool equipment (if applicable)
- [ ] Monitor AC performance — clean or replace filters
- [ ] Check for carpenter ants, termites, and other pests
- [ ] Maintain landscaping — trim trees/bushes away from house
- [ ] Inspect garage door operation and lubricate
- [ ] Touch up exterior paint
- [ ] Clean and inspect patio furniture

## Fall Checklist
- [ ] Schedule furnace inspection and tune-up
- [ ] Clean gutters (again — leaves!)
- [ ] Winterize outdoor faucets (shut off, drain, insulate)
- [ ] Winterize irrigation system (blow out lines)
- [ ] Seal gaps around windows, doors, and foundation
- [ ] Reverse ceiling fan direction (clockwise for winter)
- [ ] Test heating system before cold weather
- [ ] Stock winter supplies (ice melt, snow shovels)
- [ ] Inspect chimney and fireplace (clean if needed — hire sweep)
- [ ] Drain and store garden hoses
- [ ] Check weather stripping on all exterior doors

## Winter Checklist
- [ ] Monitor pipes in cold areas — keep cabinet doors open during freezes
- [ ] Clear snow from roof valleys and ice dams (if applicable)
- [ ] Check attic insulation
- [ ] Replace smoke detector batteries (if not hardwired)
- [ ] Plan spring projects and budget
- [ ] Check emergency kit supplies
- [ ] Monitor humidity levels (30-50% ideal)
- [ ] Inspect for drafts and seal

## Appliance Maintenance

| Appliance | Task | Frequency |
|-----------|------|-----------|
| Refrigerator | Clean coils, check door seals | Every 6 months |
| Dishwasher | Clean filter, run cleaning cycle | Monthly |
| Washing machine | Clean drum, check hoses | Monthly / check hoses annually |
| Dryer | Clean vent line thoroughly | Annually (more with heavy use) |
| Water heater (tank) | Flush sediment | Annually |
| Water heater (tankless) | Descale/flush | Annually |
| HVAC | Professional tune-up | Twice yearly (spring AC, fall heat) |
| Garbage disposal | Clean and sharpen | Monthly |
| Range hood | Clean/replace filter | Every 3-6 months |

## System Lifespan Guide

| System | Expected Lifespan | Start Planning Replacement |
|--------|-------------------|---------------------------|
| Asphalt roof | 20-30 years | At 20 years |
| HVAC system | 15-20 years | At 12-15 years |
| Water heater (tank) | 8-12 years | At 8 years |
| Water heater (tankless) | 15-20 years | At 15 years |
| Furnace | 15-25 years | At 15 years |
| Dishwasher | 8-12 years | At 8 years |
| Washer/dryer | 10-14 years | At 10 years |
| Refrigerator | 10-18 years | At 12 years |
| Garage door opener | 10-15 years | At 10 years |
| Exterior paint | 5-10 years | At 5 years (check annually) |
